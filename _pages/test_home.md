I am a first year PhD student at the Center for Atmospheric and Ocean Science at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences. My interests are in (geophysical) fluid dynamics, eddying, nonlinearity, and dynamics. 

I am grateful to the National Science Foundation's Graduate Research Fellowship for partially funding my graduate studies.

# Projects:

* My (undergraduate) thesis discusses the controls on distributions of local wave activity (a measure of the meandering of the jetstream) as well as the relation between local wave activity and weather variables. It can be found [here](https://clairevalva.github.io/files/thesis_final_cvalva.pdf), but note that the work on this is not peer reviewed. The paper relating to this work is currently in prep.
